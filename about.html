Our Mission

Runnin' Wreck is a club for all who run. Whether you have been running since the fifth grade or have only run one time in that charity 5k, there will be someone to run with. We aim to provide a welcoming community for runners of all abilities, from casual joggers to competitive racers.

Competitive Team

For those looking to compete, Runnin' Wreck participates in the National Intercollegiate Running Club Association (NIRCA). We travel to races throughout the semester, competing against other university club teams in cross country, track and field, and road races.
