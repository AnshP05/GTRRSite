Become a Member of Runnin' Wreck

Step 1: Eligibility

You must be a current student at Georgia Tech to join the club.

Step 2: Come to a Practice

The best way to get started is to show up to one of our practices! Check out the schedule to find a time that works for you. We are always excited to welcome new faces.

Step 3: Pay Dues

Dues are collected at the beginning of each semester. The amount will be announced at the first few meetings and on our social media. Dues help cover race entry fees, travel, and social events.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast do I need to be?

We welcome all paces! We have a wide range of runners, and you will always find someone to run with.

What should I bring to my first practice?

Just bring yourself, proper running shoes, and a water bottle. Be ready to run and have fun!

Are there tryouts?

There are no tryouts to be a general member of the club. Everyone is welcome to join our practices.
